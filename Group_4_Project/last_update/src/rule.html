HOW TO PLAY -or- LUDO RULES
Ludo is a strategy board game with easy rules to understand. The game's goal is to take all your 4 tokens into the 'Home' space.
Sounds simple, right?

Players choose their color: RED, YELLOW, BLUE, or GREEN.

Minimum players: 2, maximum players: 4.

Each player places their colored tokens in their respective colored squares.

Tokens are placed in the square's circles.

Players take turns rolling the dice sequentially.

Rolling a six allows a player to move a token out of the colored square to the starting position.

Tokens can be moved based on the number rolled on the dice.

Rolling a six grants an additional bonus turn.

Only one token from each player can be placed in a small box during the race.

If a player's token lands on a position with another player's token, the latter is sent back to their colored square.

To rejoin the race, the player whose token was sent back must roll a six again.

Completing the race in the anticlockwise direction allows the player to enter the matching colored line of boxes leading to the triangle.

The player must bring all four tokens into the triangle (house) to win the game.

In games with three or four players, there can be a second winner.
